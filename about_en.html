NEAR prides itself in its ability to produce high quality, photorealistic images, focusing above all in the faithful representation of the creator’s vision.

 

 

 

 

Services

From a technical drawing, photo or concept sketch, we can bring your idea to life either in image or video.

 

We’ve available to follow your project from day one. This close relation from concept to final product allows you to have a much better control over the aesthetic and functional aspects of the project, as well as saving time and work on the development.

 

We like to keep in touch, and either in person or at a distance we’re always near you.

 

  • 01   ·   Quotation

    When requesting a quotation, a clear intention of the project should be given. For us to be able to present the correct values we need some information on the project, such as CAD drawings, photos or 3D models.

  • 02   ·   Beginning of the project

    When we receive the confirmation and down payment, we will begin the project. We always provide previews throughout the whole process so as to confirm that everything is just right.

  • 03   ·   Cameras and previews

    As soon as we have the 3D scene set up, we create the camera angles and send preview images so that you can confirm them for the final renders.

  • 04   ·   Lighting and materials

    After the camera angles are approved, we’ll work on the defined materials and lighting to obtain the desired mood of the renders.

  • 05   ·   Rendering and post-production

    When the materials and lighting are finished, we’ll advance to the final photorealistic renders. After having the final HD images, we’ll send a low resolution preview for approval and comments. We allow up to 3 rounds of modifications per image.

  • 06   ·   Delivery format

    Our standard delivery format is 2500 pixels for the widest dimension of the image, with a 300 dpi resolution. For any different size, please ask us know during the quotation phase.

  • 07   ·   Delivery and invoice

    After receiving the final payment, we’ll send the final images through email or wetransfer. In the latter case, an email with instructions and download link will be sent.
